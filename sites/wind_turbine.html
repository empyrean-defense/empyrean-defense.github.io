Wind Turbine

The wind turbine site offers an affordable and efficient solution for generating power, perfect for sustaining basic operational needs at your facilities. Strategically positioned to harness natural wind energy, these turbines convert kinetic wind power into clean, renewable electricity, ensuring your sites remain functional and eco-friendly. The initial investment is minimal compared to other energy sources, making it an excellent choice for players looking to balance cost and sustainability while managing their resources effectively. With reliable performance and low maintenance requirements, the wind turbine site is a smart, cost-effective addition to your power generation strategy.

Stats

  • Hitpoints: 400 hp
  • Cost: 600
  • Power Produced: 300
