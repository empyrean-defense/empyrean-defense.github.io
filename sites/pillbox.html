Pillbox

The pillbox is a small, fortified defensive structure equipped with a rapid-fire machine gun. It excels at taking down infantry and light vehicles, making it a strong choice for defending key positions. With sturdy protection and reliable firepower, it is an effective and affordable option for stopping advancing ground and naval units

Stats

  • Hitpoints: 500 hp
  • Damage: 2
  • Range: 40m
  • Rate of Fire: 4
  • Crit Chance: 3%
  • Armor Piercing: 10%
  • Cost: 800
  • Power Consumed: N/A
