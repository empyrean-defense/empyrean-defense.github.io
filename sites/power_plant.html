Power Plant

The Power Plant is a critical infrastructure site that generates and distributes energy to power your other installations. As the backbone of your operations, it ensures that military and economic sites function efficiently by maintaining a stable energy grid. Strategically placing and protecting your Power Plants is essential, as their destruction can leave vital towers and facilities offline, leaving your defenses vulnerable.

Stats

  • Hitpoints: 800 hp
  • Cost: 1200
  • Power Produced: 600
