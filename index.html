About

Empyrean Defense is a modern warfare tower defense game where you build, upgrade, and customize defenses to hold back an enemy invasion. Learn abilities and skills, craft advanced ammunition, and gather resources on the battlefield to strengthen and expand your defense.

News

Early Access release is planned for July 24, 2025. For anyone interested in testing the game prior to Early Access please contact Nighthawk Studios.
