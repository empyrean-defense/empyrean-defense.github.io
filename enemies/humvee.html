Humvee

The Humvee (High Mobility Multipurpose Wheeled Vehicle) is a robust and versatile four-wheel-drive vehicle used by armed forces around the world. Renowned for its durability and adaptability, the Humvee can traverse diverse terrains and endure extreme conditions, making it ideal for a range of missions from troop transport to medical evacuation and reconnaissance. Equipped with advanced armor and a variety of weapon systems, the Humvee provides crucial support in both combat and peacekeeping operations, ensuring the safety and effectiveness of military personnel in the field.

Stats

  • Speed: 12 m/s
  • Armor: 20 ap
  • Credits: 150
