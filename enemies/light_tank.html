Light Tank

The light tank is an American infantry fighting vehicle designed to transport infantry into battle while providing covering fire to suppress enemy troops and armored vehicles. It is equipped with a 25mm M242 Bushmaster chain gun, a TOW missile launcher, and a 7.62mm coaxial machine gun. The Bradley also has a robust armor system to protect its crew and passengers from small arms fire and artillery fragments. Additionally, it includes advanced electronics, including thermal imaging for night operations, and a communication system to enhance battlefield coordination. The M2 Bradley can carry a crew of three and up to six infantry soldiers, offering both firepower and troop mobility on the modern battlefield.
