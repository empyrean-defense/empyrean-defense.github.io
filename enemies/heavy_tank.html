Heavy Tank

The heavy tank is a third-generation American main battle tank designed for modern armored ground warfare. It features advanced composite armor, a powerful 120mm smoothbore cannon, and a multifuel turbine engine, providing a top speed of around 45 mph. The tank's sophisticated fire control system and thermal imaging allow it to engage targets effectively in any weather conditions and at long ranges.
