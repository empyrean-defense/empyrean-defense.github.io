Tactical Support Jet

The tactical support jet is an armored, low-flying aircraft known for its devastating ground attacks. Equipped with a powerful rotary cannon and an array of missiles, it excels at breaching defenses with precision strikes. Its durable design and ability to withstand significant damage make it a formidable adversary, requiring players to strategically position their towers to counter its relentless assaults. The tactical support jet's combination of speed, firepower, and resilience challenges players to adapt quickly to its aggressive tactics.

Stats

  • Health: 150 hp
  • Speed: 20 m/s
  • Armor: 15 ap
  • Credits: 150
